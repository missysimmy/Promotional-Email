When times are uncertain, it helps to know there are certain things you can still control. Like making sure your beneficiary information is up to date. A regular review is always a good idea — especially when life cirumstances change.

We've made it easy to manage beneficiaries with your MassMutual account. Just log in, and review or add your beneficiary's information:

  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Date of Birth
  • Social Security number (strongly recommended)

Get started by following the link in this email, or visit MassMutual.com. Once you're logged in, simply click on the Beneficiary section.

The more we know about your beneficiaries, the better we can help you take care of them. Check off this simple to-do today.

Did you know?

Even when on-track with planning, many make the mistake of failing to update their beneficiaries for their retirement accounts (IRAs and 401(k)s), life insurance policies and annuities. That can be an expensive oversite.

It's important to remember that the beneficiary forms for such accounts are legally binding documents, which supersede whatever is stated in your will.
